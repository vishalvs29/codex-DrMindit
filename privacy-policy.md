# Privacy and Data Policy

## What we collect
- Email and profile data from your Clerk account (email, name, profile image).
- Mood check-ins, journal text, sleep and stress values, tags, and user preferences.
- Chat session history and metadata needed to personalize AI wellness support.
- Billing subscription state and payment metadata required to manage premium access.
- Usage metadata for system performance, rate limiting, and support.

## How we use your data
- To personalize wellness recommendations, mood analytics, and guided programs.
- To support the AI chat experience and keep conversation context for the current session.
- To manage premium subscriptions and entitlement for Stripe-billed features.
- To protect service availability with rate limiting and abuse prevention.

## How long we keep your data
- Your wellness entries and chat history are retained until you request deletion.
- Billing and subscription records are kept for as long as your account is active and for legal compliance.
- Logs and rate-limit metadata are retained only as long as necessary for security and troubleshooting.

## Your choices
- You can delete your data by contacting support or using the account deletion flow if available.
- You can opt out of premium features by cancelling your subscription through Stripe.
- You can remove your account at any time; this will delete your wellness and chat data from our system.

## Data security
- We use encrypted database storage and third-party providers only for authentication, AI, and payment processing.
- Sensitive credentials are stored in environment variables and are not committed to version control.
- We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Applicable law
- This policy is designed to comply with India's DPDPA 2023 and GDPR principles where applicable.
- If you are an EU user, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your data.

## Contact
If you have questions about this policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please contact the site administrator.
